Week 1: Reading Comprehension – Analysing Author's Purpose, Viewpoint, and Language Techniques

Learning Objectives

  • Analyse author's purpose and viewpoint in texts
  • Identify and evaluate language techniques used to convey meaning
  • Understand how language choices influence reader response

In-Class Activities

  • Read a non-fiction article or persuasive text
  • Discuss the author's purpose and viewpoint
  • Analyse language techniques such as rhetorical questions, emotive language, and statistics
  • Group discussion: How do these techniques persuade the reader?

Homework Assignments

  • Read a newspaper article or opinion piece
  • Write a paragraph analysing the author's purpose and viewpoint
  • Identify two language techniques and explain their effect

Assessment Details

End-of-week quiz on author's purpose and language techniques.

  • Question 1: Analyse the author's viewpoint in a given text
  • Question 2: Identify and explain language techniques
  • Question 3: Evaluate how language influences the reader
