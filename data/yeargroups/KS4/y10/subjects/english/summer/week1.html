Week 1: Reading Comprehension – Summarising, Evaluating Texts, Comparing Author Techniques and Viewpoints

Learning Objectives

  • Summarise key points from texts accurately
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of texts and author techniques
  • Compare viewpoints and techniques across different texts

In-Class Activities

  • Read two contrasting texts (e.g., fiction and non-fiction)
  • Summarise the main ideas of each text
  • Evaluate the author's techniques and viewpoints
  • Compare how the authors convey their messages

Homework Assignments

  • Read two short articles on a similar topic
  • Write a summary of each and compare the authors' viewpoints
  • Evaluate which text is more persuasive and why

Assessment Details

End-of-week quiz on summarising and evaluating texts.

  • Question 1: Summarise a given text in your own words
  • Question 2: Evaluate the effectiveness of author techniques
  • Question 3: Compare viewpoints in two texts
