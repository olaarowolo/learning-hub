Topic: Transition to KS3 English

Introduction

This week focuses on introducing students to the expectations and skills required for KS3 English. Pupils will explore a range of texts and begin developing analytical and creative writing skills.

We will cover reading comprehension, annotation techniques, and narrative writing basics.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the transition from KS2 to KS3 English.
  • Develop annotation and reading comprehension skills.
  • Begin narrative writing with clear structure.
  • Explore vocabulary and grammar for effective writing.

In-Class Activities

  • Read and annotate a short story extract.
  • Group discussion on narrative elements.
  • Write a short narrative paragraph.

Homework

Write a short story paragraph using descriptive language and clear structure. Bring it to class for peer review.
