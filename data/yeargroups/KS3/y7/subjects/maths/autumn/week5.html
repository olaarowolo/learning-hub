Topic: Division

Introduction

This week, we tackle division. You'll learn reliable methods for both short and long division, understand how to handle remainders, and use divisibility tests to find factors quickly. We'll apply these skills to solve problems involving sharing and grouping.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Short & Long Division: Confidently use formal methods to divide up to a 4-digit number by a 2-digit number.
  • Interpreting Remainders: Express remainders as whole numbers, fractions, or decimals depending on the context.
  • Divisibility Tests: Learn shortcuts to test if a number is divisible by 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 9, and 10.
  • Problem Solving: Solve word problems that require division, such as sharing items equally or scaling down quantities.

In-Class Activities

Let's practice division with these interactive tasks:

  • Divisibility Card Sort: Quickly sort numbers into categories based on their divisibility.
  • Real-World Scenarios: Solve problems about arranging bus seating, forming teams, or packaging items into boxes.
