Topic: Fractions

Introduction

This week is all about fractions! We will learn how to simplify them, convert between different types, and perform all four operations: adding, subtracting, multiplying, and dividing. These are essential skills for many areas of maths.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Simplifying Fractions: Finding the simplest form of a fraction by dividing the numerator and denominator by their highest common factor.
  • Improper & Mixed Numbers: Converting between improper fractions (e.g., 17/5) and mixed numbers (e.g., 3 2/5).
  • Adding & Subtracting: Finding common denominators to add and subtract fractions.
  • Multiplying & Dividing: Learning the methods for multiplying and dividing fractions.
