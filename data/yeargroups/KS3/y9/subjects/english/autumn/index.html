Autumn Term Overview

Focus: Reading comprehension, narrative writing, grammar, and punctuation

This term builds your analytical reading skills and develops your creative writing abilities. You'll explore inference and deduction techniques, craft compelling narratives, master complex grammar structures, and refine your punctuation skills. The term culminates with extended narrative writing and speaking/listening activities.

  • Week 1

    Reading Comprehension – Inference

    Making inferences and drawing conclusions from texts
  • Week 2

    Reading Comprehension – Deduction

    Identifying themes and viewpoints in texts
  • Week 3

    Narrative Writing – Plot Development

    Creating engaging story structures
  • Week 4

    Narrative Writing – Characterisation

    Developing complex and believable characters
  • Week 5

    Grammar – Complex Sentences

    Using subordinate clauses and relative clauses
  • Week 6

    Grammar – Conjunctions & Stylistic Devices

    Advanced conjunctions and language techniques
  • Week 7

    Punctuation – Semi-Colons & Colons

    Using advanced punctuation for clarity
  • Week 8

    Punctuation – Dashes, Brackets & Ellipses

    Advanced punctuation techniques
  • Week 9

    Writing Extended Narratives – Climax

    Building tension and creating dramatic peaks
  • Week 10

    Writing Extended Narratives – Resolution

    Crafting satisfying story endings
  • Week 11

    Speaking & Listening – Presentations

    Delivering effective presentations
  • Week 12

    Speaking & Listening – Debates

    Participating in structured debates
