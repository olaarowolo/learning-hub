Topic: Place Value & Number Systems

Introduction

This week, we dive into the fundamentals of our number system. Understanding place value is the key to working confidently with large numbers, decimals, and negative numbers. We'll explore how to read, write, compare, and round numbers effectively.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Representing Numbers: Learn to write numbers up to 1,000,000 in both digits and words.
  • Comparing & Ordering: Develop skills to compare and order integers and decimals.
  • Rounding: Master rounding to the nearest 10, 100, 1,000, and to a given number of decimal places.
  • Negative Numbers: Understand and use negative numbers in context, such as on a number line or with temperature.

In-Class Activities

Get ready for some hands-on practice with these fun activities:

  • Place Value Bingo: A fun game to test your ability to recognise numbers written in words.
  • Temperature Comparison: Use real-world examples to work with negative numbers.
  • Quick-fire Rounding Challenge: Test your rounding skills against the clock!
