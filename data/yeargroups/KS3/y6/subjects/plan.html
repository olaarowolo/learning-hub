year 6 English Curriculum Plan (by Week)

Autumn Term

  • Week 1: Introduction to year 6 English & Diagnostic Writing
  • Weeks 2–6: Novel Study: "A Monster Calls" by Patrick Ness
  • Weeks 7–8: Poetry from Other Cultures
  • Week 9: Creative Writing: Short Stories
  • Week 10: Grammar and Punctuation Focus
  • Week 11: Public Speaking and Debating Skills
  • Week 12: Assessment & Revision

Spring Term

  • Weeks 1–5: Shakespeare Study: "A Midsummer Night's Dream"
  • Weeks 6–8: Non-Fiction and Media Texts
  • Weeks 9–11: Persuasive Writing and Speeches
  • Week 12: Assessment & Revision

Summer Term

  • Weeks 1–5: Modern Play Study: "The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time"
  • Weeks 6–8: Dystopian Fiction and Creative Response
  • Weeks 9–11: End of Year Project: Creating a Zine
  • Week 12: Assessment & Revision
