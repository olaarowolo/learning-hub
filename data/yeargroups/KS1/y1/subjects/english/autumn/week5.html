Topic: Writing Simple Sentences

Introduction

This week, we focus on writing simple sentences. A sentence is a group of words that makes sense and tells us something. We'll learn to write sentences with capital letters and full stops.

Remember to start with a capital letter and end with a full stop!

Learning Objectives

  • Write simple sentences with correct punctuation.
  • Use capital letters at the start of sentences.
  • Use full stops at the end of sentences.
