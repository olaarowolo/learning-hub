Topic: Sequencing Events in a Story

Introduction

This week, we focus on sequencing events in a story. Understanding the order of events helps us retell stories correctly and improves our comprehension.

Remember, stories have a beginning, middle, and end!

Learning Objectives

  • Identify the sequence of events in a story.
  • Retell a story orally in the correct order.
  • Use sequencing words like first, then, next, finally.
