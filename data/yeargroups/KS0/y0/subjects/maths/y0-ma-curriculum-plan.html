Year 0 Maths Curriculum Plan

Overview

This page outlines the curriculum plan for Year 0 Mathematics, providing a structured overview of the topics and skills to be covered throughout the year.

Key Areas of Focus

  • Number and Place Value: Understanding numbers to 20, counting, and ordering.
  • Addition and Subtraction: Basic addition and subtraction within 20.
  • Shape, Space and Measures: Recognizing and naming common 2D and 3D shapes, understanding simple measures.
  • Problem Solving: Applying mathematical reasoning to solve simple problems.

Term Breakdown

  • Autumn Term: Number and Place Value, Introduction to Addition and Subtraction.
  • Spring Term: Addition and Subtraction, Shape and Space.
  • Summer Term: Measures, Problem Solving, Consolidation of concepts.
