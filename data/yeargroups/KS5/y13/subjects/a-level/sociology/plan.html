Year 13 A-Level Sociology Curriculum Plan (by Week)

Autumn Term

  • Week 1: Introduction to Crime & Deviance
  • Weeks 2–6: Theories of Crime & Deviance
  • Weeks 7–8: Social Control and Punishment
  • Week 9: Patterns & Trends in Crime
  • Week 10: White-Collar & Corporate Crime
  • Week 11: Research Methods in Crime
  • Week 12: Applying Theory to Case Studies

Spring Term

  • Weeks 1–2: Beliefs in Society - Introduction & Functionalism
  • Weeks 3–4: Marxism & Feminism in Religion
  • Week 5: Secularisation
  • Weeks 6–7: New Religious Movements & Social Change
  • Weeks 8–9: Applying Theory to Case Studies
  • Week 10: Research Methods in Religion
  • Week 11: Revision & Essay Practice
  • Week 12: End-of-Term Assessment

Summer Term

  • Weeks 1–2: Recap of Sociological Theories
  • Weeks 3–4: Research Methods Recap
  • Week 5: Applying Theory to Topics
  • Weeks 6–7: Evaluating Sociological Research
  • Week 8: Essay Writing Skills
  • Weeks 9–10: Past Paper Practice
  • Week 11: Mock Exam
  • Week 12: Final Review & Targeted Practice
