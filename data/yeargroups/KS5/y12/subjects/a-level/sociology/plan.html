Year 12 A-Level Sociology Curriculum Plan (by Week)

Autumn Term

  • Week 1: Introduction to Sociology
  • Weeks 2–6: Research Methods
  • Weeks 7–8: Key concepts: society, culture, socialisation, norms, values
  • Week 9: The role of sociology in understanding social behaviour
  • Week 10: Types of research: qualitative vs quantitative
  • Week 11: Methods: surveys, experiments, interviews, observations, content analysis
  • Week 12: Sampling techniques, ethical considerations, reliability, validity, representativeness

Spring Term

  • Weeks 1–2: Education - Role of education in society (functionalism, Marxism, feminism)
  • Weeks 3–4: Educational achievement: factors affecting attainment (class, gender, ethnicity)
  • Week 5: The education system: policies, inequalities, and debates
  • Weeks 6–7: Applying theory to education
  • Weeks 8–9: Research methods in education
  • Week 10: Contemporary issues in education
  • Week 11: Revision and essay practice
  • Week 12: Assessment & Revision

Summer Term

  • Weeks 1–2: Families and Households - Types of families and household structures
  • Weeks 3–4: Roles and relationships within families
  • Week 5: Social policies affecting families
  • Weeks 6–7: Contemporary issues: changing family patterns, domestic roles, divorce
  • Weeks 8–9: Applying theory to families
  • Week 10: Research methods in families
  • Week 11: Revision and essay practice
  • Week 12: End of Year Assessment & Revision
