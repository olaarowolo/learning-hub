Activity Overview

Use number lines to explore numbers up to 1,000. This activity helps learners understand larger sequences and develop place value skills.

Instructions

  • Count forwards and backwards in ones, tens, and hundreds up to 1,000.
  • Fill in missing numbers in number lines extending to 1,000.
  • Practice identifying patterns and sequences in larger numbers.
  • Challenge: Count in steps of 25, 50, or 100 along the number line.
