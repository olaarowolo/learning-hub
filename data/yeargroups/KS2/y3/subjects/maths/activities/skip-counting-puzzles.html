Lesson Note

Understanding Skip-Counting Through Puzzles

Puzzles help you practice skip-counting in a fun and challenging way, improving pattern recognition and problem-solving skills.

  • Complete sequences with missing numbers.
  • Find patterns in unusual or irregular sequences.
  • Challenge yourself to make your own puzzles for independent learning.

Tip: Write down your solutions and explain your thinking to strengthen understanding.

Activity Overview

Practice skip-counting with number puzzles. Complete the missing numbers in each sequence and check your answers interactively.

Instructions

  • Observe the sequence and the step size.
  • Fill in the missing numbers.
  • Use logic and pattern recognition to solve the puzzles.
