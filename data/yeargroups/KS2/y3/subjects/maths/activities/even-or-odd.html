Lesson Note

Understanding Even and Odd Numbers

  • Even numbers are divisible by 2 with no remainder (0,2,4...).
  • Odd numbers leave a remainder of 1 when divided by 2 (1,3,5...).
  • Practice recognising patterns independently to strengthen understanding.

Activity Overview

Determine whether each number is even or odd.
