Lesson Note

Understanding Skip-Counting by 8

Skip-counting by 8 helps you recognise patterns and builds a foundation for multiplication and division by 8.

  • Example: 8, 16, 24, 32, 40 …
  • Notice that each number increases by 8.
  • Practice using objects in groups of 8 to visualise patterns.

Tip: Count aloud and write your own sequences. This helps independent learning.

Activity Overview

Practice skip-counting by 8 to strengthen number sense and prepare for multiplication facts.

Instructions

  • Count aloud in steps of 8 up to 100 or beyond.
  • Fill in missing numbers in sequences below.
  • Try counting objects in sets of 8 in real life.
