Lesson Note

Understanding Skip-Counting by 100

Skip-counting by 100 helps you understand hundreds, place value, and prepares you for addition and subtraction with large numbers.

  • Example: 100, 200, 300, 400, 500 …
  • Notice each number increases by 100.
  • Try grouping objects into sets of 100 to visualise patterns.

Tip: Write sequences and count aloud. This encourages independent learning and number sense.

Activity Overview

Practice skip-counting by 100 to reinforce understanding of hundreds and number patterns.

Instructions

  • Count aloud in steps of 100 up to 1000 or beyond.
  • Fill in the missing numbers in sequences below.
  • Use real-life objects, like counting pages in a book or coins in bundles of 100.
