Activity Overview

Practise counting forwards up to 1,000. This activity helps learners build confidence with larger numbers and sequences.

Instructions

  • Count forwards in ones, tens, and hundreds starting from any number below 1,000.
  • Complete sequences where some numbers are missing.
  • Challenge: Count forwards in steps of 25 or 50 to improve speed and fluency.
  • Try real-life counting: e.g., counting items in hundreds or large sets.
