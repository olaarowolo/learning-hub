Multiplication Tables Basics

  • Multiplication is repeated addition.
  • Multiplication tables help us quickly calculate totals in groups.
  • Independent learning: try writing your own examples with everyday items.

Example: 3 √ó 4 = 12 means 3 groups of 4 items = 12 items total.

Key facts:

  • Any number √ó 0 = 0
  • Any number √ó 1 = itself
  • Multiplication is commutative: 3 √ó 4 = 4 √ó 3
