Week 1: Introduction to Narrative Writing

In this week, students will explore the basics of narrative writing, focusing on story structure, characters, and setting.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the elements of a narrative story.
  • Identify characters, setting, and plot in stories.
  • Begin writing simple narrative sentences.

In-Class Activities

  • Read a short story and discuss its elements.
  • Create character profiles for a story.
  • Write a short paragraph describing a setting.

Homework

Write a short story using the elements learned in class. Focus on including a clear beginning, middle, and end.

Assessment

Students will submit their short story for review. Feedback will focus on narrative structure and creativity.
