Topic: Addition & Subtraction

Introduction

This week, we build on our number skills by mastering addition and subtraction. We'll explore quick mental strategies and formal written methods to solve problems accurately and efficiently, including those involving decimals and multiple steps.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Mental Strategies: Using techniques like compensation and partitioning to calculate in your head.
  • Column Method: Applying formal column addition and subtraction for large numbers.
  • Working with Decimals: Adding and subtracting numbers with up to three decimal places.
  • Multi-Step Word Problems: Breaking down complex problems into smaller, manageable steps.

In-Class Activities

We'll put your skills to the test with these practical activities:

  • Shopping Receipt Challenge: Calculate totals and change using real-life shopping scenarios.
  • Error-Spotting: Become a detective and find the mistakes in a series of calculations.
