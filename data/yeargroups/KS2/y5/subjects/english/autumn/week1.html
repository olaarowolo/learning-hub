Reading Comprehension - Retrieving Information

Focus on understanding texts by finding and recalling key information explicitly stated in the text.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Identifying main ideas and supporting details.
  • Answering questions that require direct retrieval from the text.
  • Distinguishing between fact and opinion.

In-Class Activities

Get ready for some hands-on practice with these fun activities:

  • Text Treasure Hunt: Read a passage and find specific information like names, dates, or facts.
  • Question Quest: Answer retrieval questions in pairs, discussing your answers.
  • Fact or Opinion Game: Sort statements from a text into fact or opinion categories.
