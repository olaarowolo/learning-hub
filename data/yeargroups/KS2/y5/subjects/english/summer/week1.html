Reading Comprehension - Summarising Main Ideas

Focus on identifying and condensing the key points from longer texts.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Identifying the main ideas in a text.
  • Distinguishing main ideas from supporting details.
  • Writing concise summaries in your own words.

In-Class Activities

Practice summarising with these engaging activities:

  • Summary Pyramid: Read a passage and create a pyramid of summaries from detailed to concise.
  • Main Idea Match: Match main ideas to supporting details from a text.
  • Group Summaries: Work in groups to summarise different sections of a longer text.
