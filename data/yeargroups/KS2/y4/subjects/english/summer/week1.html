Reading Comprehension - Summarising Main Ideas

Focus on identifying and condensing the key points from a text.

  • Read a longer passage or story.
  • Identify the main ideas and supporting details.
  • Write a short summary in your own words.

Assessment

Write a summary of a short story, focusing on the main ideas and key events.
