Reading Comprehension - Retrieving Information

Focus on understanding texts by finding and recalling key information.

  • Read a short story or passage carefully.
  • Answer questions about specific details in the text.
  • Discuss the main ideas with a partner or group.

Assessment

Complete a worksheet with questions based on a short text to demonstrate understanding.
