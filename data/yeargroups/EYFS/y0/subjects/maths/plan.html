Weeks 1‚Äì3: Numbers and Counting Foundations

Focus: Counting to 5, recognising numerals 0‚Äì5.

Activities:

  • Counting everyday objects (toys, snacks, natural items).
  • Number songs/rhymes (e.g., "5 little ducks").
  • Matching numerals to quantities.
  • Sorting and comparing small sets (more/less, same).

Key Vocabulary:

one, two, three, more, less, same, count.

Weeks 4‚Äì6: Building to 10

Focus: Numbers 6‚Äì10, ordering and comparing.

Activities:

  • Ten frames for number recognition and counting.
  • Ordering numbers on a washing line (0‚Äì10).
  • Counting forwards and backwards to 10.
  • Begin simple addition using objects (e.g., 3 + 2).

Key Vocabulary:

first, last, next, before, after, add, altogether.

Weeks 7‚Äì9: Shapes, Space & Patterns

Focus: Shape recognition and spatial awareness.

Activities:

  • Recognise and name 2D shapes (circle, square, triangle, rectangle).
  • Explore patterns (ABAB using colours, sounds, actions).
  • Building with 3D shapes (blocks, junk modelling).
  • Positional language (under, next to, behind).

Key Vocabulary:

circle, square, triangle, rectangle, pattern, behind, in front.

Weeks 10‚Äì12: Developing Number Sense

Focus: Numbers beyond 10, early problem solving.

Activities:

  • Counting to 20 with confidence.
  • One more / one less games.
  • Sharing and grouping (link to halving/sharing).
  • Simple subtraction using real objects.
  • Revisiting patterns and extending them.

Key Vocabulary:

more than, fewer than, one more, one less, double, share, half.

üìä Assessment & Review

Ongoing: Observe children during play and group activities.

End of 12 Weeks: Check progress towards:

  • Confident counting to 10 (and beyond).
  • Recognising numerals to 10+.
  • Understanding one more/less.
  • Beginning to add and subtract practically.
  • Recognising and using patterns.
  • Using shape and positional vocabulary.
