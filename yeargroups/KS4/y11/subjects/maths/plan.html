Year 11 Maths Curriculum Plan (UK)

Year 11 Maths focuses on consolidating GCSE topics, problem-solving, and exam preparation. Students develop skills in algebra, geometry, statistics, and number theory, with emphasis on applying knowledge to real-world problems and exam techniques.

Autumn Term

  • Algebra: quadratic equations, simultaneous equations, inequalities
  • Geometry: circle theorems, transformations, Pythagoras' theorem
  • Statistics: data representation, averages, probability
  • Exam practice and revision strategies

Spring Term

  • Advanced algebra: sequences, functions, graphs
  • Trigonometry: sine, cosine, tangent rules
  • Further geometry: volume and surface area, vectors
  • Mock exams and targeted revision

Summer Term

  • Final exam preparation and practice papers
  • Problem-solving and reasoning skills
  • Review of key topics and exam techniques
