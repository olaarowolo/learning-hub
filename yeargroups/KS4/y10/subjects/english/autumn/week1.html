Week 1: Reading Comprehension – Inference, Deduction, Identifying Themes, Motifs, and Viewpoints

Learning Objectives

  • Understand how to make inferences and deductions from texts
  • Identify key themes, motifs, and author viewpoints
  • Analyse how language techniques support meaning

In-Class Activities

  • Read an extract from a novel (e.g., "To Kill a Mockingbird" or similar)
  • Discuss inferences about character motivations and themes
  • Group activity: Identify motifs and how they develop the viewpoint
  • Practice deduction exercises with short passages

Homework Assignments

  • Read Chapter 1 of assigned novel
  • Write a short paragraph inferring the main character's viewpoint
  • Identify one theme and one motif in the chapter

Assessment Details

End-of-week quiz on inference and deduction techniques.

  • Question 1: Infer the author's viewpoint from a given passage
  • Question 2: Identify themes and motifs in a short story
  • Question 3: Deduce character relationships from dialogue
