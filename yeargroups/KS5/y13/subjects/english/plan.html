Year 13 English A-Level Curriculum Plan (by Week)

Autumn Term

  • Week 1: Introduction to Year 13 & Shakespeare's 'King Lear'
  • Weeks 2–6: 'King Lear': Tragedy, Power, and Family
  • Weeks 7–9: NEA Coursework: Proposal and Research
  • Week 10: Critical Theory Application
  • Week 11: NEA Coursework: Drafting and Development
  • Week 12: Assessment & Revision

Spring Term

  • Weeks 1–5: Modern Drama: 'A Streetcar Named Desire'
  • Weeks 6–8: Poetry Collection: Carol Ann Duffy's 'Feminine Gospels'
  • Weeks 9–11: Unseen Prose and Poetry Analysis Skills
  • Week 12: Mock Exams & Revision

Summer Term

  • Weeks 1–4: Synoptic Revision: Connecting Texts
  • Weeks 5–8: Exam Technique and Timed Practice
  • Weeks 9–11: Final Revision & A-Level Examinations
  • Week 12: Post-Exam Project (TBC)
