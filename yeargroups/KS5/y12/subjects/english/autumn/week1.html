  • Discussion on what makes a text 'literary'

Section A: Key Concepts (10 marks)

  1. Define 'literary theory' in your own words. (2 marks)
  2. What is the difference between 'theme' and 'motif'? (2 marks)
  3. Explain the term 'narrative voice'. (2 marks)
  4. Give an example of 'foreshadowing' from a text you have read. (2 marks)
  5. What does 'context' mean in literary analysis? (2 marks)

Section B: Text Analysis (10 marks)

  1. Analyse the use of imagery in the following sentence: "The sun dipped below the horizon, painting the sky in hues of crimson and gold." (3 marks)
  2. Identify the tone in this excerpt: "With a heavy heart, she turned away, knowing that this was the end." (2 marks)
  3. Discuss how the author's choice of words contributes to the atmosphere. (3 marks)
  4. What literary device is used here: "Time flies like an arrow"? (2 marks)
