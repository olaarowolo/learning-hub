Week 1: Introduction to Sociology

← Back to Autumn Term

Learning Objective

Understand sociology as a subject; explore what society and culture are.

Topic Introduction

Sociology studies society, social behaviour, and social structures. Key terms: Society, Culture, Norms, Values, Socialisation.

Key Terms

  • Society: A group of people living together with shared rules and culture.
  • Culture: Norms, values, beliefs, and traditions shared by a society.
  • Norms: Expected behaviours.
  • Values: Core beliefs guiding behaviour.
  • Socialisation: Process of learning norms and values.

In-Class Activities

  • Mind map: "What makes up a society?"
  • Class discussion: Examples of norms in school vs home.
  • Group activity: Identify cultural differences in a global context.

Homework Assignments

  • Read chapter on introduction to sociology.
  • Write a short paragraph on why sociology is important.
  • Prepare examples of norms and values from your life.
