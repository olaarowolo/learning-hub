Understand Negative Numbers

← Back to Activities Index

Activity Overview

Learn to understand and work with negative numbers. These interactive activities will help you count, compare, and solve problems involving numbers below zero.

Instructions

  • Complete each sequence including negative numbers.
  • Compare numbers and identify which is smaller or larger.
  • Try simple addition and subtraction with negative numbers.
