Counting Patterns - up to 1,000

← Back to Activities Index

Activity Overview

Practice recognising and continuing counting patterns up to 1,000. This helps strengthen number sense and prepares learners for multiplication and division.

Instructions

  • Identify sequences in steps of 2, 5, 10, 50, and 100.
  • Fill in missing numbers in patterns up to 1,000.
  • Create your own number sequences and challenge a partner to complete them.
  • Observe patterns in hundreds, tens, and units for better place value understanding.
