Year 3 Maths: Multiplication Tables

Today we will practice multiplication tables. Multiplication is repeated addition. Multiplication tables help us quickly calculate how many items we have when we group them.

Example: 3 √ó 4 = 12 means 3 groups of 4 items = 12 items total. Or: 4 + 4 + 4 = 12

Key facts to remember:

  • Any number multiplied by 0 equals 0
  • Any number multiplied by 1 equals the number itself
  • Multiplication is commutative: 3 √ó 4 = 4 √ó 3
