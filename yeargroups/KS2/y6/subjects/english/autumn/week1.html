Week 1 - Reading Comprehension Basics

← Back to Autumn Term

Learning Objectives

  • Identify main ideas and themes in a text
  • Understand summarising and retrieval techniques
  • Develop skills in reading comprehension

In-Class Activities

Read a short story or passage and discuss the main ideas. Practice summarising the key points.

Group activity: Identify themes and retrieve specific information from the text.

Homework

Read a provided text at home and write a summary of the main ideas.

Answer retrieval questions based on the text.

Assessment Details

This week's assessment focuses on reading comprehension skills.

Assessment Questions

  1. What is the main idea of the passage?
  2. Identify two key themes discussed in the text.
  3. Summarise the story in 3-5 sentences.
  4. Retrieve the following information: [specific details from text].
