Reading Comprehension - Making Inferences

Focus on understanding texts by reading between the lines and making predictions.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Reading texts carefully to infer meaning beyond the literal.
  • Making predictions about events or characters.
  • Justifying inferences with evidence from the text.

In-Class Activities

Engage in these activities to practice inference skills:

  • Inference Detective: Read passages and identify implied meanings.
  • Prediction Challenge: Predict what might happen next in a story and discuss.
  • Evidence Hunt: Find textual evidence to support your inferences.
