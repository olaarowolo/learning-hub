Topic: Multiplication

Introduction

This week is all about multiplication! From recalling your times tables to tackling long multiplication with large numbers and decimals, you'll strengthen a crucial mathematical skill. We'll also see how multiplication is used to solve real-world problems.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Multiplication Facts: Recalling and applying multiplication facts up to 12 Ã— 12 with speed and accuracy.
  • Long Multiplication: Mastering the formal written method for multiplying numbers up to 4 digits by a 2-digit number.
  • Multiplying Decimals: Learning how to multiply decimal numbers by whole numbers.
  • Word Problems: Applying multiplication to solve problems involving area, scaling, and cost.

In-Class Activities

Get ready to multiply with these engaging activities:

  • "Speed Grid" Challenge: Race against the clock to fill out a multiplication grid.
  • Recipe Scaling: Figure out how much of each ingredient you need if you double, triple, or halve a recipe.
