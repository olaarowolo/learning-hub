Welcome to Year 9 English!

Year 9 English builds on your previous learning and prepares you for GCSE-level study. You'll develop advanced analytical, reading, writing, and speaking skills through a diverse range of fiction, non-fiction, and poetry texts. This year focuses on critical thinking, sophisticated language use, and confident communication.

Our curriculum combines literary analysis with creative expression, helping you understand authorial techniques, craft compelling narratives, and articulate ideas persuasively. You'll explore complex themes, refine your grammar and punctuation, and build a rich vocabulary for academic and creative writing.

Key Skills You'll Develop

  • Critical Reading: Analyse texts deeply, identify authorial intent, and evaluate different viewpoints
  • Creative Writing: Craft sophisticated narratives with complex characters, plots, and themes
  • Academic Writing: Write formal essays, reports, and persuasive texts with clarity and precision
  • Grammar & Language: Master advanced punctuation, sentence structures, and stylistic techniques
  • Communication: Present confidently, debate effectively, and express ideas persuasively

Assessment & Progress

Throughout the year, you'll complete regular assessments to track your progress and prepare for GCSEs. These include reading comprehension tests, creative writing tasks, formal essays, grammar exercises, and speaking presentations. Your work will be marked against clear success criteria, with opportunities for improvement and redrafting.

GCSE Preparation

Year 9 builds essential foundations for GCSE English Language and Literature. You'll develop the analytical skills needed for exam questions, practice different writing styles, and learn to structure responses effectively. Regular practice with past papers and exam-style questions will help you feel confident and prepared.
