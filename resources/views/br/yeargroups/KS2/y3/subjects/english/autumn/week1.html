Week 1: Reading Comprehension - Main Ideas

← Back to Autumn Term

Topic: Understanding Main Ideas in Texts

Introduction

This week, we will focus on developing your reading comprehension skills by learning how to identify the main ideas in a text. Understanding the main idea helps you grasp the overall message the author wants to convey.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify the main idea of a paragraph or passage.
  • Distinguish between main ideas and supporting details.
  • Summarize the main points in your own words.

Activities

Read short passages and highlight the sentences that express the main idea. Practice summarizing paragraphs with a partner or in your notebook. Discuss how supporting details help explain the main idea.
